# Summarisation

## Purpose
Condense long documents, articles, or conversations into a concise summary.

## Guidelines
1. Preserve the **key facts**, decisions, and action items.
2. Keep the summary to roughly **20 % of the original length** unless told otherwise.
3. Use bullet points for scannability.
4. Highlight any **open questions** or unresolved topics at the end.

## Output Format
- **Title**: A one-line headline.
- **Summary**: Bullet-point summary.
- **Open Questions**: Items still needing resolution.
